Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

This tool ("osu! Score Retriever") is an open-source project designed to interact with the osu! API.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data on our servers. All data processing happens locally or in transit solely for the purpose of retrieving scores from the osu! API.

  • Client Credentials: Your Client ID and Client Secret are sent to the osu! API to obtain an access token. We do not store these on our servers. They may be saved in your browser's Local Storage for your convenience.
  • Search Queries: The usernames and search parameters you enter are used to query the osu! API and are not logged.

Third-Party Services

This application communicates directly with the osu! API. By using this tool, you are subject to the osu! Terms of Service.

Cookies and Local Storage

We use your browser's Local Storage to save your API credentials so you don't have to re-enter them. You can clear this at any time by clearing your browser data.

Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, please check the project repository.
